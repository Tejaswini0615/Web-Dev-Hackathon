About VIT Chennai

"A place to learn, a chance to grow"

We are a group of ardent tech enthusiasts with the zeal to learn, to build, and a thirst to be the best. A small group of passionate techies to explore different domains of technology has now blossomed into the official Robotics club of VIT, Vellore. Our mission is to spread knowledge about robotics by organizing various free workshops and seminars that help people out in finding their technical niches and build their own projects.

Our members collaborate on some incredible projects and nurture glorious teams that represent us all over the world, and have several accolades to their name. With pride, all RoboVITians try to live up to the legacy left by our predecessors.
